How to Download YouTube Videos

  1. Copy the link of the YouTube video you want to download.
  2. Paste the video URL into the "Enter the video URL" field of our tool.
  3. Select your desired format and quality (MP4 video or MP3 audio).
  4. Press the "Download" button and get your file in seconds.

Limits and Recommendations

Our tool is designed to offer an efficient service, but it is important to consider the following limitations:

  • Maximum video duration: Videos longer than 50 minutes cannot be processed.
  • Conversion time: Converting a video usually takes 1-2 minutes, depending on its size and duration.

Recommendation: For a smoother experience, we suggest processing shorter videos or those that do not require additional conversion.
